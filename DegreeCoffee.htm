Kumbakonam coffee has its unique taste which has, first the "aroma" the smell.

It has a smell which creates a mood to consume it immediately.

Its speciality is the use of cows' milk without any adulterants.

Why it is called as Degree Coffee?

In the olden days, milk that was certified as pure with a lactometer was called “degree milk”, and coffee that was prepared with this milk became popular as “degree coffee”.
