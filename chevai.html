Temple for Mars (Chevvai) This is one of the navagraha temples and is dedicated for Mars. The temple is huge and very ancient built by Cholas atleast 1000-1500 years ago. It is believe by praying to Angarahan here he relieve you from all the dosham from chevvai.

Location:Mahalakshmi Illam,Vivekananda Nagar,Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu 612001

Main god:Lord Vaidhiyanathar with ThaiyalNayagi.

Significance:

The temple is known for the sanctum of chevvai. It has three great deities-lord Murugan as Muthukumarsamy, Vaidyanathar and mother Thayyalnayagi. Siva is worshipped only after the midnight pujas are offered to Muthukumarsamy.
