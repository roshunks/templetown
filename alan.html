This is temple is dedicated to Jupiter / Guru Baghawan; located near Kumbakonam. One of the most powerful Navagraha temples. Better to visit the temple before 8 am so that crowd is less and you can have good dharshan as well. Oldest and very powerful temple dedicated Guru Baghawan. Please plan to visit if you are in and around of Kumbakonam.

Location:Sivan S St, Alangudi, Tamil Nadu 612801

Main god: Abathsagewarar (Shiva), Dakshinamoorthy (Guru or Jupiter)

The temple is open from 6 .00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. and 4.00 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.
